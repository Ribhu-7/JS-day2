datatypes:
  1. Number: contains integer and float values, i.e., decimal values are also taken along with numbers.
  2. String: anything which is present inside double quotes , can be characters , words , sentences , address , etc
  3. Boolean: can either be true or false value.
keywords:
  1. var: can be declared & initialised in same line , 2 or more variables with same name possible , can be redeclared & reinitialised
  2. let : can be declared in a line with a value in another line , can be also declared & initialised in same line , cannot be redeclared , but can be reinitialised , cannot have 2 variables with same name in let
  3. const: can be declared & initialised in same line, cannot be redeclared or reinitialised.
